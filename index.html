Areas of Expertise

Surgical Specialisations

Comprehensive neurosurgical care across cranial, spinal, and functional domains with the latest minimally invasive techniques.

🧠
Brain Tumour Surgery

Awake craniotomy, stereotactic biopsy, and image-guided resection for benign and malignant intracranial neoplasms with maximal safe resection.

🦴
Complex Spine Surgery

Minimally invasive spinal fusion, disc replacement, and deformity correction for cervical, thoracic, and lumbar conditions.

🩸
Cerebrovascular Surgery

Microsurgical clipping of aneurysms, AVM resection, and carotid endarterectomy under advanced neuromonitoring.

⚡
Functional Neurosurgery

Deep brain stimulation (DBS) for Parkinson's disease, epilepsy surgery, and neuromodulation for chronic pain.

👶
Paediatric Neurosurgery

Specialised care for hydrocephalus, congenital malformations, and childhood brain tumours.

🎯
Neuro-Oncology

Multidisciplinary approach to primary and metastatic brain tumours with oncology and radiation therapy teams.
