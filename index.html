Areas of Expertise

Surgical Specialisations

Comprehensive neurosurgical care with a dominant focus on spine surgery, neurotrauma, and brain tumors — utilising advanced intraoperative technologies and minimally invasive techniques.

🦴
Degenerative Spine Surgery

High-volume specialist in slip disc, sciatica, cervical and lumbar degenerative conditions — the core 60% of surgical practice with excellent recovery outcomes.

🔬
Endoscopic Spine Surgery

Advanced keyhole endoscopic techniques for disc herniation, spinal canal stenosis, and nerve compression — minimal tissue damage, faster recovery.

🧠
Brain Tumour Surgery

Awake craniotomy and fluorescence-guided tumour resection for safe maximal removal — preserving critical brain functions with advanced neuromonitoring (IONM).

🚑
Neurotrauma

Emergency and high-acuity head injury management — representing ~20% of the surgical case mix. Full ICU neurosurgical care and emergency intervention.

⚡
Intraoperative Neuromonitoring (IONM)

Real-time monitoring of neural function during complex cranial and spinal surgeries — ensuring maximum safety and reducing post-operative deficits.

🎯
Advanced Neuro-Imaging & Navigation

Intraoperative MRI (ioMRI) and neuronavigation-guided surgery for precise targeting in complex cranial and spinal procedures.
